Shop

Welcome to our Farm Shop. Here at our Farm Shop we sell the very best meat and local produce, ensuring our customers get the highest quality possible. Our family have been farming the same land for over 300 years and run a mixed stock farm, with welfare being our top priority.

Mixed Stock

We are a mixed stock farm and they sell a range of meat boxes and hampers, with the meat prepared by their own master butcher. It's free range and ethically reared and slaughtered too.

Veg and Fruit

We like to source fruit and vegetables from as close to home as possible (we try and keep it to a ten mile radius), which allows us to maintain high quality stock and our shelves in this department. Where possible we source our produce direct from local farmers.

The Farm Shop

We have hand-selected over 3,000 products with a real focus on Welsh, local and organic. Here you’ll find a memorable foodie experience at one of the largest organic farms in the UK. If you are en route to or from the beauty of Snowdonia, secluded Peninsula coastline, then why not break up the journey by stopping at the Estate Farm Shop to stretch your legs and browse our wide range of artisan products.

Our butchers counter is stocked daily with Organic meat – which is supplied to many of the finest hotels and restaurants around the world. There is something for everyone, whether you want a simple supper or a French-trimmed Organic Rack of Welsh Lamb. Our deli counter offers a range of Welsh, British and continental cheeses as well as charcuterie, roasted meats, pies and quiches.

The shop also offers a wide selection of beers, wines and spirits, the latest Estate Wild Beauty skincare collection, and a wide range of other gifts
